# Business Templates

Pre-built canvas templates for common business types. Use these as starting points — customize domains, systems, and connections to match the user's actual business.

## How to use templates
Ask the user what type of business they're building, then follow the matching template structure. You don't need to copy it exactly — adapt names, metrics, and connections to their specific needs.

## SaaS Startup
**Domains (5):** Awareness → Acquisition → Activation → Revenue → Retention
**Systems (10):** Content Marketing, Paid Ads → Landing Pages, Lead Nurture → Onboarding, Product Tours → Billing, Upsells → Customer Success, Churn Prevention
**Flow:** Linear left-to-right funnel
**Colors:** purple → blue → green → amber → red

## Building a custom canvas
If none of the templates match, ask the user:
1. What are the main departments/areas of your business?
2. What are the key processes in each area?
3. How does value flow between processes?

Then build domains → systems → connections → metrics following canvas-workflow.md.
